Audience

My goal here is to create a website, and eventually an app, that is based on one of Nintendo's most famous and popular games, “The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild”. In this video game, there are these creatures called Korok's that are hidden all over the map. It is your job to find them. In return for finding them, they give you a seed that you can use later in-game. What this website will allow you to do is to find the general location of these “Korok's” all over the world via geographic location, similar to geocaching. Your job is to go to these locations and find a QR code that has been placed by someone else previously, scan it, and then collect the rewards. Do you want to go on an adventure in search of these Korok's?

Purpose of the First Child Page: How the App Works

• Show how the app is supposed to work with details about what mechanics that are built into it.
• Show what the person needs to do to find and collect the Korok

This app in many cases is like geocaching. There are QR codes that are hidden around, and your job is to find them! You open the app up on your phone you view where there may be potential Korok's. When your location is nearby a potential Korok, you will receive a little notification with a sound that says “A Korok is nearby!” and then the hunt begins. You walk around with the sound getting louder when you get close, and quieter when you are farther away. When you find the location of the QR code, a scanner will pop up and then you scan the code. A video will play that says “Yahaha! You found me!” and then you will receive a little Korok seed that you can use later!
